Sew It Brand Guidelines

Our mission

Sew what?

The mission on Sew It is to teach people how to sew and tailor their own clothes. We make sewing tutorial videos, host classes, and provide materials for what you want to create. We also have sewing machine techs at all our stores for all your machine maintenence needs. We here at Sew It want to help people make their visions come to life. In a world of fast fashion we want to slow things down a bit so that you can have the skills to make long lasting clothing that you will love. We also encourage tailoring your clothing to have a better fit, fix holes, and fix rips.

The theme of Sew It is creativity, self sufficiency, perseverance, and learning.
